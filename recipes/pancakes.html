Pancakes

Description

Make super simple thin pancakes in portions of 8. In only 20 minutes and with 4 ingredients you can get a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • milk
  • salt

Steps

  1. Add 2 eggs, 250g flour, 500ml milk and a pinch of salt to a bowl.
  2. Mix until you have no more lumps.
  3. Heat a bit of butter in a frying pan, when the pan is warm enough pour in one full ladle of batter and spread it out over the frying pan.
  4. Bake the pancake for around 2-3min until the upside is dry. Turn it around and bake it for another 1-2min.
  5. Put the pancakes that are done on a plate and put another plate upside down on top of it to keep them warm.
  6. Enjoy!
