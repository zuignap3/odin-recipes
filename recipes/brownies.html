Brownies

Description

The perfect recipe for the fudgiest brownies! only takes 15 min to prepare and 35 min bake time for a yield of 24 servings.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup of butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 and a half cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Combine sugar, melted butter, and vanilla in a large bowl. Beat in eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition, until thoroughly blended.
  3. Sift flour, cocoa powder, and salt into a separate large bowl. Gradually stir flour mixture into egg mixture until blended; stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Spread batter evenly into the prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 35 to 40 minutes.
  6. Let cool completely before slicing into 24 squares.
  7. Enjoy!
